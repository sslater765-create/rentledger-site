Frequently asked questions

Is RentLedger a subscription?

No. RentLedger Pro is a one-time purchase. Buy it once and it's unlocked forever, no monthly or annual fees.

Where is my data stored?

Entirely on your iPhone. No account, no servers, nothing sold. Optional iCloud sync uses your own private iCloud.

How do I move data to a new phone?

Use your normal encrypted iPhone/iCloud device backup, or the in-app Back Up All Data and Restore From Backup.

I bought Pro but it's not showing after reinstalling.

Open Settings in the app and tap Restore Purchase. Your unlock is tied to your Apple ID and restores for free.

What is Schedule E?

The IRS form landlords use to report rental income and expenses. RentLedger sorts entries into the right Schedule E categories and generates a clean PDF for your tax preparer.

Does the receipt scanner send my receipts anywhere?

No. Scanning runs on your device using Apple's built-in tools. Receipts never leave your phone.

Is this tax advice?

No. RentLedger is a record-keeping tool. Always review your numbers with a qualified tax professional.
