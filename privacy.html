RentLedger Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 5, 2026

RentLedger is built to keep your financial information private. The short version: it stays on your device, and we never see it.

What we collect

Nothing. RentLedger does not collect, transmit, sell, or share any personal or financial information. We have no servers that receive your data and no analytics or tracking tools inside the app.

Where your data lives

Everything you enter is stored locally on your iPhone. It never leaves your device unless you explicitly choose to share or export a file, or turn on optional iCloud sync, which uses your own private iCloud.

Receipt scanning

Receipt scanning and text recognition happen entirely on your device using Apple's built-in frameworks. Receipt images are never uploaded anywhere.

Purchases

The one-time RentLedger Pro unlock is processed by Apple through the App Store. We never see your name, card, or payment details.

Notifications

Reminders you enable are scheduled as local notifications on your device. No reminder data is sent to us or anyone else.

Children

RentLedger is intended for adults and is not directed at children under 13.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, we will update the date above and post the revised version at this URL.
